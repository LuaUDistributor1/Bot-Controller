**Commands:**

**!goto [user]** - Teleports bot instantly to the chosen target.

**!loopgoto [user]** - Repeatedly teleports bot to the chosen target. (kinda annoying)

**!unloopgoto**	- Stop loop teleport.	

**!spin [speed]**	- Makes bot spin (default speed 10).

**!unspin**	- Stop spinning.

**!follow [user]** - Makes your bot follow the chosen target.

**!unfollow**	- Stop following.	

**!float [height]**	- Makes bot float (default 10).

**!unfloat** - Stop floating.

**!say [message]** - Makess your bot send a chat message.

**!lineup**	- Makes your bot stand in line with you.	

**!view [user]** - Makes bot account spectate chosen target. (Pretty useless tbh)

**!unview**	- Stops spectating.	

**!sit** - Bot sit.	

**!stand** - Bot stand.	

**!refresh** - Kill bot.	

**!rejoin**	- Speaks for itself.

**!cmds** - Bot says command list in chat
